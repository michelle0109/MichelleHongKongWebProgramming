Tourist spots

Hong Kong offers lots of great places for you to travel and explore! 3 main ones include:

Victoria Peak (left Image)

  1. Known for its beauitful view, this spot gives you a perfect view of the city.

  2. Continous view of tall skyscapers embedded between rivers and mountains.

  3. Try to catch the view during sunset or even check out the nightview.

Avenue of stars (Center Image)

  1. Located in Tsui tam, this spot is right along the river giving you a skyline view along with river boats.

  2. You can see golden statues of famous celebrties such as Bruce Lee all along the boardwalk.

  3. If you have time, also check out the clock tower or one of the museums located here!

Go explore with a quick fary ride! (Right image)

  1. Hong Kong is home to over 1000 islands and a large body of water, making boat rides a common transporation.

  2. Tai o(Bottem right) showcases the fisherman life in Hong Kong. Looking for a challenge? Vist the big budda at the hilltop by climcing up a large flight of stairs!

  3. Cheung Chau Island (bottem left) is where endless street food and beautiful beaches await. You can also vist the famous pirate cave near by!
